PANTRY STOCKING

We curate a selection of delicious, local, nutritious foods so your employees can look forward to coming in every day and doing their best work.The core of our services is keeping your office pantry stocked. We supply a variety of snacks, fresh fruits and veggies, dairy products, and more to fit your office’s unique needs, habits, and preferences. We come to your office 3-5 times per week to re-stock, tidy up, and keep tabs on what’s popular with your staff.

AN ONGOING RELATIONSHIP

Nothing about your office is one-size-fits-all. We get to know your company, your employees, and your office on a personal basis and seek out the perfect selections for you.We provide a completely personalized service with ongoing conversations between us, your office management team, and your employees. Every time we come by to re-stock your pantry, we note what’s popular and what didn’t go over as well, listen to feedback, and adjust our selections to match.

SPECIAL EVENTS

When you want a fun, festive, and memorable event without the fuss of a full catering service, we provide tailor-fit food and drink.Whether it’s an office birthday, happy hour, client visit, or low-key holiday party, we’re here to help. We can source everything from custom birthday cakes to breakfast platters to caviar trays.

OUR PROCESS

We want to get to know your business, your employees, and your office. We start every engagement with a site visit where we meet your staff, learn more about their routines and what they want from your office pantry, and establish a budget and restocking schedule to fit your needs.

Then, we curate a unique selection of items to fit your office. Keeping lines of communication with your employees open, we continuously re-evaluate our selections and rotate products weekly to keep it fun, interesting, and exciting. We’ll come by your office to re-stock, collect feedback, and keep everything neat and tidy every few days.
